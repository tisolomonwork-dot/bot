# **App Name**: AetherMind Trading

## Core Features:

- Data Aggregation: Fetch and consolidate real-time trading data from Bybit and Gemini.
- Portfolio Overview: Display a combined portfolio value with asset breakdown, PNL, and exchange splits.
- AI Market Analysis: Provide concise summaries of market conditions, leveraging AI to highlight potential opportunities, risks and recommended actions. Acts as a tool by helping the user choose how much relevant data is incorporated.
- Actionable Insights: Present prioritized, suggested actions for the user (e.g., reduce position, monitor breakout) with associated risk scores and confidence meters.
- Market Scanners & Alerts: Implement momentum, reversal, and breakout scanners, delivering AI-summarized signals based on custom user-defined rules.
- Positions and Orders Tracking: Monitor positions with entry price and PNL, as well as open orders with side, price, and status details.
- AI-Powered Chat Interface: Allow users to ask specific questions to the AI regarding market trends, portfolio adjustments, and strategy evaluations.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep royal blue (#4169E1), providing a sense of trust and stability.
- Background color: Light grey (#F0F8FF), a clean, professional backdrop for content.
- Accent color: Vibrant sky blue (#87CEEB), used sparingly for highlighting key metrics and interactive elements.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif offering a modern, neutral appearance suitable for both headlines and body text.
- Use a set of crisp, line-based icons that complement the overall minimalist aesthetic.  Color-code icons to represent various asset classes or risk levels for clarity.
- Employ a card-based layout to compartmentalize information, ensuring that key metrics and AI insights are immediately visible and easily accessible. Prioritize a mobile-responsive design to facilitate access across various devices.
- Incorporate subtle micro-animations for user interactions to enhance the dynamic feel and intuitive appeal without detracting from its professional veneer.
